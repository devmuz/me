01 / Overview

Who We Are

Mohammad Mozammil, operating under the brand mz.dxbapps, is an independent mobile app developer based in Dubai, UAE. This Privacy Policy governs all applications published by mz.dxbapps on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains what data our apps collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights as a user. Where an individual app collects additional or different data, a supplementary notice will be provided within that app.

Short version: We collect only what we need to make our apps work. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
02 / Data We Collect

Information We Collect

The data we collect depends on the specific app and features you use. Generally, our apps may collect the following categories of information:

Category Examples Purpose
Account Data Name, email address, username Authentication & profile
Device Data Device model, OS version, unique device ID Crash reporting, compatibility
Usage Data Features used, session duration, in-app actions App improvement, analytics
Location Data Approximate or precise location Only when required by app features
Payment Data Transaction ID, purchase status In-app purchase validation (no card data stored)
Communications Support messages, feedback Customer support

We collect only the minimum data necessary for the app to function. If an app does not require a feature (e.g. location), it will not request that permission.

03 / How We Use Data

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the features of our applications
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts securely
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To send important app-related notifications (updates, security alerts)
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To understand how users interact with the app and improve the experience
  • To respond to support requests and user feedback
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to serve advertisements, build marketing profiles, or sell to any third party.

04 / Third-Party Services

Third-Party Services We May Use

Some of our apps integrate third-party SDKs and services to power core functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, crash reporting, and analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy ↗
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — In-app purchases and distribution. Governed by Apple and Google's respective policies.
  • Stripe / RevenueCat — Payment processing for subscriptions. Card details are never seen or stored by us.
  • Google Maps SDK — Location-based features where applicable. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Sentry / Crashlytics — Crash and error reporting to improve stability.
Note: Each individual app page on the App Store / Play Store lists the specific third-party SDKs used by that app. Check the app's store listing for the most accurate list.
05 / Data Storage & Retention

Where Your Data Lives

Data may be stored on your device locally and/or on secure cloud servers (primarily Google Firebase infrastructure). Servers are located in the United States and/or EU regions depending on the app.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service. If you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

Analytics and crash data are retained in anonymized or aggregated form and may be kept longer for product improvement purposes.

06 / Children's Privacy

Children Under 13

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at mz.dxbapps@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Apps specifically designed for children will include a separate, age-appropriate privacy notice compliant with COPPA and relevant local regulations.

07 / Your Rights

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdrawal of consent — Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mz.dxbapps@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Users in the EU/EEA are covered under GDPR; California residents are covered under CCPA.

08 / Security

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest
  • Firebase Security Rules limiting data access to authorised users only
  • Regular dependency updates to patch known vulnerabilities
  • No storage of raw payment card details — handled exclusively by PCI-compliant processors

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please disclose it responsibly to mz.dxbapps@gmail.com.

09 / Policy Changes

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our apps, legal requirements, or best practices. When we do, we will revise the Last Updated date at the top of this page.

For significant changes, we will notify users through the app or via email (where we have your address). Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. The current version is always available at this URL.
